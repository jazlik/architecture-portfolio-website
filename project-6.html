CLOUD

Location: Warsaw, Poland

Date: 2019

The project itself serves in the development of digital fabrication skills. The idea is to use different fabrication methods in order to replicate the model of the Cloud Gate.

At first the virtual 3D model of the Cloud Gate was created. All of its parts were parametrically designed by means of optimisation in software like Rhinoceros 3D and Grasshopper. On account of that, each joint, rod or spandex is unique.

There are a few materials and methods used for the total assembling of the model. The methods used are 3D printing, casting and laser cutting. The materials used are thermoplastic filament, silicone mass, metal rods and spandex.
